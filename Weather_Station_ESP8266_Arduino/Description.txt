Weather Station Project - consisting of the Adruino Uno platform and the ESP280 wifi module.
Uses 2xDHT11, DHT22, BMP280 sensors, soil moisture sensor.
It allows you to test the temperature, humidity, pressure, altitude, soil moisture.
The wifi module is powered by pins from Adruino, Adruino is powered by AA batteries.
The data is sent to the Internet platform thanks to ThingSpeak.
